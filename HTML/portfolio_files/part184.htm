Economic factor model The model of stock returns in which the stock return may be determined primarily by economic factors. In this model, the expected stock return equals the product of the factor premiums and the stock’s exposure to the factors.

Efficient-market hypothesis The hypothesis that security prices reflect all avail- able information and cannot be predicted.
