Writing my first business proposal

I am writing a business proposal about Multi-modal LLM+Household Robot. It sucks.

The first draft is pathetically horrible

This proposal comes from some random thoughts when preparing for the group meeting. After reading multiple papers using iGibson as simulation environment and conduct indoor semantic mapping, I realized that it may be just the time of proposing a universal household robot that can understand household environments and make interactions. Moreover, no company has released their solutions-even they’ve done everything in the Tech stack.

Everything was done by myself. I quickly drew a system overview and roughly understand what is required. By using the images in papers, I showed what I expect LLM, the central processor need to do. the technical part is nearly done.

However, I have no idea about business. The business model and market investigation were as perfect as two pcs of remains. They are just rough structures with no detail, no formatting, and no human intelligence is included.

In all,

  • This is a proposal completely started on a whim, with nothing but passion prepared.
  • I have no human team members, AutoGPT is my CTO.
  • No business background, no marketing background.
  • No prior verification, no real world demo, just some thoughts and simulations.
  • I don’t know how will my proposal cost and where will the investigation (Though nearly impossible to get) go.

The second draft…

I changed the entire structure.

  • Market analysis of smart households and what’s the next step of its development.
  • How will AIGC contribute to the development.
  • Product introduction (Nearly the same)
  • How much investigation do I need to get, and how will the fund be used.
  • How will my business run. (Still incomplete)

For market analysis, I was told to refer to give a definition of the household robot market and refer to similar markets. I try to prove that the market of household robotics has a huge potential by stating the demand of “3rd” generation of smart households, and the rising market size of service robots and smart households.

I shall prove the potential by individual needs too…
